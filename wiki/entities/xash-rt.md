# xash-rt

Xash3D FWGS fork with real-time path tracing (C / C/C++). Centers on OpenGL rendering and modding of the Xash3D/GoldSrc-compatible engine stack—useful for graphics programmers and rendering researchers studying path-traced remasters and engine render paths rather than cheat or anti-cheat artifacts.
