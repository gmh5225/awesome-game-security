# battleye-rust

Rust library for the [[battleye]] RCON (Remote Console) UDP protocol: packet encoding with checksum hashing, socket I/O, and examples for listening to and reading/writing remote-console commands. Useful for game-server admins and researchers studying BattlEye’s server-management interface rather than kernel/UM client detection. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/steffalon__battleye-rust.md)

Complements client/tooling samples such as [[arma3beclient]] and kernel/UM BE research ([[blindeye]], [[be-shellcode]]) with a protocol-facing RCON angle.
