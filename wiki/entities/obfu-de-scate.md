# Obfu-DE-Scate

Python Android APK deobfuscation helper: analyzes obfuscated class/method names, emits human-readable mappings, and builds HTML reports with class-hierarchy diagrams plus method classification. Aimed at Android reverse engineers and malware analysts facing ProGuard/R8-obfuscated APKs (`[Apk]` lane). (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/user1342__Obfu-DE-Scate.md)

Complements apktool/jadx decode workflows — name/mapping recovery and visual triage, not a full unpacker or native-code deobfuscator.
