# Proxmox

Community **PVE Helper Scripts** for Proxmox VE: dialog-box installers and setup helpers that automate common host/LXC/VM tasks on a Proxmox lab. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/tteck__Proxmox.md)

Useful for game-security researchers and reverse engineers who need a repeatable QEMU/KVM/PVE research host in the `Cheat > QEMU/KVM/PVE/VBOX` lane—adjacent to guest-fingerprint work such as [[proxmox-ve-anti-detection]] / [[qemu-anti-detection]], but focused on host provisioning rather than anti-detection.
