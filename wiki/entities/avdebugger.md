# avdebugger

Tool that automatically recovers antivirus (AV) signatures so researchers can study how scanners match binaries. Aimed at game-security and reverse-engineering work in the Cheat → **Anti Signature Scanning** lane—understanding signature coverage and crafting mutations that avoid static AV hits. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/scrt__avdebugger.md)

Complements signature-mutation samples such as [[lumina-cheat]], Authenticode transplant tooling such as [[sigthief]], and IDA signature-scan/create plugins such as [[ida-fusion]] / [[yarascan-ida]].
