# miniDumpReader

Python-focused minidump / memory-analysis tooling for offline dump inspection. Aimed at anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers working Windows kernel dump analysis adjacent to AC RE. Complements RAM-image frameworks such as [[volatility]] / [[volatility3]] and WinDbg-flavored `MEMORY.DMP` workflows such as [[ephemera]].
