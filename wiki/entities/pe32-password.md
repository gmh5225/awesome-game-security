# pe32-password

C/C++ PE32 password-oriented binary packer listed under Anti Cheat → Binary Packer. Aimed at anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers studying packed/modded PE clients rather than shipping as an AC product. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/ytk2128__pe32-password.md)

Useful as a packer/password-protection reference alongside other PE triage and obfuscation tooling—not a full unpacker or debugger.
