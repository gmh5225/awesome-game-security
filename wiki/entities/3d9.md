# 3d9

Developer-oriented DX11 tooling to fix broken stereoscopic effects in DirectX 11 games. Aimed at graphics programmers and Windows game-tooling developers who fix bugs, add features, or document usage—not an end-user product build. Useful for studying DX11 stereo / Present-adjacent render pipelines. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/visotw__3d9.md)
