# Waldo

Deep-learning research project aimed at **triggerbot and aimbot detection**. The current build is CS2-specific and requires a user-trained model. Useful for anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers studying the `Detection:Triggerbot & Aimbot` lane (behavioral / ML signals against AI visual cheats). (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/waldo-vision__waldo.md)
