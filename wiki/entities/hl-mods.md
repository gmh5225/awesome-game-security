# hl-mods

Collection of Half-Life / GoldSrc modifications by Doug "Surreal" Arcuri and contributors (solidi). Spans late-1990s/early-2000s historical mod sources through active Cold Ice Remastered workspaces with CI packaging for GoldSrc mods. Useful for Source/GoldSrc modders and researchers studying Half-Life mod architecture rather than cheat or anti-cheat artifacts. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/solidi__hl-mods.md)
