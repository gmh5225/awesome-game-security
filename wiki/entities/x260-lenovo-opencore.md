# x260-lenovo-opencore

Hackintosh OpenCore EFI for the Lenovo ThinkPad X260 (OpenCore 0.8.5 / Big Sur era): ACPI patches, kexts, and config covering Intel HD 520, Realtek audio, Intel WiFi, trackpad, and power management so macOS can boot on that hardware. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/x90skysn3k__x260-lenovo-opencore.md)

Useful as a non-Apple macOS research host for game-security / RE testing—not a cheat or anti-cheat component. Adjacent to other EFI-surface study ([[xigmapper]]) but aimed at a working macOS lab rather than early-load driver mapping.
