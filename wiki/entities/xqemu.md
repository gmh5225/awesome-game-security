# xqemu

QEMU-based emulator for **original Xbox games**: full-machine software emulation without requiring hardware virtualization support. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/xqemu__xqemu.md)

Useful for game-security researchers and reverse engineers studying offensive techniques in the `Cheat > QEMU/KVM/PVE/VBOX` lane—adjacent to anti-detect guests such as [[qemu-anti-detection]] / [[proxmox-ve-anti-detection]], but focused on Xbox title playback rather than fingerprint spoofing.
