# EscapeFromTarkov-Trainer

Discontinued Escape From Tarkov (EFT) trainer after about five years of maintenance. With the game’s 1.0 release and switch to [[il2cpp]], internal modding became substantially different and more complex, so the project stopped rather than rewrite for the AOT backend. Useful historically for researchers studying offensive techniques in the cheat / game:eft lane (Mono-era internal patterns vs post-IL2CPP surface). (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/sailro__EscapeFromTarkov-Trainer.md)
