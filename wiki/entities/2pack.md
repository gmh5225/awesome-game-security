# 2Pack

Rust-based PE and shellcode packer supporting EXE/DLL PE files and raw shellcode. Aimed at anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers studying packed clients / payloads under Anti Cheat → Binary Packer, rather than shipping as an AC product. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/xM0kht4r__2Pack.md)

Useful as a Rust packer reference alongside C/C++ PE32/X64 packing and PE triage tooling—not a full unpacker or debugger.
