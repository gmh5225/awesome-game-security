# loldrivers-client

Go and PowerShell client for scanning LOLdrivers (known-abused / living-off-the-land signed drivers). Aimed at game-security and RE researchers in the cheat / vulnerable-driver lane—complements HVCI-oriented inventory such as [[hvci-loldrivers-check]] and blocklist operators such as [[solemn]].
