# FightingGame-UE5

UE5 fighting-game sample with a comprehensive animation stack: root-motion locomotion, combat idle transitions, and directional movement blending. Blueprints/UAssets cover walk, run, jump, attack, hit reaction, block, dodge, and death sequences organized by movement direction and combat state; montage-based combat moves, input handling, and game-mode configs sit on UE5 Animation Blueprints. Aimed at game developers and UE5 researchers studying fighting-game animation architecture and root motion—not a cheat or anti-cheat artifact. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/stackOverflower92__FightingGame-UE5.md)

Complements other Unreal Game Develop surfaces in the wiki ([[action-roguelike]], [[simple-fps-template]], [[epic-survival-game]], [[ue5-with-dear-imgui]]) by focusing on fighting-game anim BP / root-motion combat rather than FPS, roguelike, or ImGui plugin wiring.
