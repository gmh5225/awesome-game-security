# anticuckoo

Research project focused on **Cuckoo** sandbox / virtual-environment detection. Crash PoCs are demonstrations only. Aimed at anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers in the `Detection:Virtual Environments` lane. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/therealdreg__anticuckoo.md)

Complements guest-fingerprint / hidden-VM research such as [[qemu-anti-detection]] and [[proxmox-ve-anti-detection]], and HV-presence probes such as [[checkhv-um]] / [[hypervisor-detection]].
