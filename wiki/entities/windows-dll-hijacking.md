# windows-dll-hijacking

Database and research catalog of Windows DLL hijacking surface: hundreds of executables and vulnerable DLL search paths covering relative-path / search-order hijacking, sideloading, and phantom DLL loads. Includes automated scanning tooling and structured vulnerability data for red-team, audit, and defender mapping of the Windows load-path attack surface. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/wietze__windows-dll-hijacking.md)

Useful as a reference for Cheat → DLL Hijack lanes and for AC/EDR researchers who track image-load / search-order abuse (not a game-specific cheat). Companion disclosed-opportunity catalog: [[hijacklibs]].
