# hook-buster

C/Python research repo under anti-cheat `Detection:hook` for studying hook integrity and hook-busting techniques. Aimed at anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers evaluating inline/IAT/vtable-style hooks rather than shipping a full commercial detector. Complements offensive trampoline/hook libraries ([[polyhook]], [[polyhook-2-0]], [[detoursnt]], [[skiphook]]) and Present-specific checks such as [[present-hook-detection]].
