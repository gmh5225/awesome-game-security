# tinyraytracer

Brief computer graphics / rendering course (ssloy). Not intended as production or physically realistic renderer code—aimed at graphics programmers and rendering researchers studying ray-tracing fundamentals in the Renderer lane. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/ssloy__tinyraytracer.md)

Companion educational surface to soft-raster [[tinyrenderer]]: ray tracing rather than a from-scratch raster pipeline; still not a GPU Present hook or cheat overlay.
