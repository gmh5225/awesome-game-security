# vac3-dumper

VAC3-oriented dump tool for modules that load at different times. Useful for game-security researchers and reverse engineers studying Valve Anti-Cheat under the cheat / explore anticheat system:vac lane. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/x1tan__vac3-dumper.md)

Companion research surface to [[vac3-inhibitor]] (hooking / memory analysis): this repo focuses on capturing VAC modules for offline RE rather than runtime inhibition.
