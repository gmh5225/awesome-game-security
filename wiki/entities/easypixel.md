# EasyPixel

Magisk module that disguises a rooted Android device as a Google Pixel (build/prop spoof). Useful for game-security and reverse-engineering workflows in the Cheat Magisk lane—device-fingerprint / Play Integrity–adjacent research, Unity mobile titles, and studying how anti-cheat or attestation stacks treat Pixel-branded props. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/the-dise__EasyPixel.md)

Sits alongside other Magisk modules such as [[move-certificate]] and install paths such as [[cheese]], opposite Magisk-detection samples like [[magiskdetector]]. Framework home: [[magisk]].
