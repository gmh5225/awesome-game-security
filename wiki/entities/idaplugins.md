# IDAPlugins

Curated integration of essential IDA Pro plugins for deobfuscation, binary diffing, and custom cryptography support. Aimed at game-security researchers and reverse engineers in the cheat / IDA Plugins lane studying offensive static RE techniques. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/ssmugabi__IDAPlugins.md)

Collection-style packaging of IDA-side tooling rather than a single-purpose plugin—complements focused plugins such as [[ida-easy-life]], [[idadeflat]], and [[xrefsext]].
