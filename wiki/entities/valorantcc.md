# ValorantCC

Valorant-oriented **crosshair setting** utility: client-side crosshair configuration for Riot’s Valorant. Assets and endpoints referenced by the project are Riot-owned; the repo is framed for researchers studying offensive techniques in the cheat / game:valorant lane rather than as a general AC product. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/weedeej__ValorantCC.md)

Sits beside per-title Valorant research such as [[valorant-dumper]] under [[vanguard]], but scoped to crosshair config rather than UE offset/SDK dumps.
