# STP

Simple Theorem Prover — an efficient SMT solver for bitvector and array theories. Used as a constraint-solving backend in binary analysis, symbolic execution, and verification workflows. Speaks SMT-LIB 2 and drives SAT backends such as MiniSat and CryptoMiniSat. Sits in the Cheat Mixed boolean-arithmetic / constraint-solver lane alongside MBA simplify/synthesize tools. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/stp__stp.md)
