# vt-debuuger

C/C++ research project for hacked hypervisor testing: kernel-level work, driver development, and plugins aimed at stressing virtualization abuse surfaces that anti-cheat systems try to detect. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/zxd1994__vt-debuuger.md)

Useful for anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers mapping VT-x/AMD-V–style hypervisor threats and `Detection: Hacked Hypervisor` tooling—not a production AC component.
