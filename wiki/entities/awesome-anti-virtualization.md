# awesome-anti-virtualization

Curated awesome list of resources on **anti-virtualization** techniques — detecting and evading virtualized environments. Aimed at anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers in the `Detection:Virtual Environments` lane. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/theo-abel__awesome-anti-virtualization.md)

Index hub rather than a single detector binary; complements sandbox/VE demos such as [[anticuckoo]], guest-fingerprint research such as [[qemu-anti-detection]] / [[proxmox-ve-anti-detection]], and HV-presence probes such as [[checkhv-um]] / [[hypervisor-detection]].
