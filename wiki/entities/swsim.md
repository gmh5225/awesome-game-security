# swsim

Pure-software **USIM/UICC SIM card simulator**: full APDU/SIM logic without physical hardware; PC/SC via swICC reader; pairs with SIMtrace2/cardem for phone testing. Covers GSM/LTE auth (MILENAGE), private LTE/IoT, and cellular security research in the cheat / Cellular·SIM lane. C/C++. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/tomasz-lisowski__swsim.md)
