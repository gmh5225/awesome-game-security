# LoudSunRun

Stack-spoofing sample that builds **synthetic call frames**, drawing on namazso, SilentMoonWalk, and VulcanRaven techniques. Aimed at game-security researchers and reverse engineers studying offensive stack presentation in the cheat / spoof-stack lane—useful alongside simpler return-address swaps such as [[return-address-spoofer]] when reasoning about `Detection:Spoof Stack` unwind consistency.
