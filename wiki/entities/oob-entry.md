# oob_entry

iOS 3.0–10.3.4 `tfp0` kernel exploit (C/C++), focused on kernel-level work and memory analysis in the cheat / iOS jailbreak research lane. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/staturnzz__oob_entry.md)

Useful for game-security and reverse-engineering study of historical iOS kernel privilege primitives (`task_for_pid(0)` / tfp0) that underpinned older jailbreak and memory-access workflows—not a modern production jailbreak.
