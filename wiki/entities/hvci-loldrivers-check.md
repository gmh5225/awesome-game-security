# HVCI-loldrivers-check

Trail of Bits PowerShell tooling that checks LOLdrivers (living-off-the-land / known-abused signed drivers) in the context of [[hvci]] (Memory Integrity). Aimed at game-security and RE researchers studying [[byovd]] / vulnerable-driver offense in the cheat lane—complementary to blocklist operators such as [[solemn]].
