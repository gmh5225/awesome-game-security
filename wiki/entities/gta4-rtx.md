# gta4-rtx

GTA IV RTX Remix compatibility mod that adapts The Complete Edition for NVIDIA’s RTX Remix path-traced remaster pipeline. Ships a custom Remix runtime, Ultimate ASI Loader integration, light translation, dynamic emissives, and wetness/rain effects; complements FusionFix and related PBR asset packs. Useful for DirectX / Windows game-tooling and graphics-compatibility research. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/xoxor4d__gta4-rtx.md)
