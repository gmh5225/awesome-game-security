# ida-ios-helper

IDA plugin to ease reversing iOS projects. Requires vtable symbols to be present. Aimed at game-security researchers and reverse engineers in the cheat / IDA Plugins lane studying offensive iOS techniques. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/yoavst__ida-ios-helper.md)

Not a general iOS unpacker—scoped to IDA workflows that already have vtable symbols available.
