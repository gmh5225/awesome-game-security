# Voidmaw

Research project centered on **VEH + `PAGE_GUARD`**: exception-driven page protection used to keep flagged or sensitive code off the radar of antivirus scanners. Aimed at anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers studying page-protection / VEH anti-tamper patterns. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/vxCrypt0r__Voidmaw.md)

Pairs with related page-protection samples such as [[no-access-protection]] (`PAGE_NOACCESS` + VEH / single-step) and VEH-chain dump tooling such as [[veh-dumper]].
