# WindEffects

Modular C++23 game engine with a full editor, runtime, and project-template system. Rendering backends cover Vulkan and DirectX 12; shaders use HLSL for deferred/PBR lighting, plus post-processing (bloom, TAA, SSAO, SSR), volumetric clouds/fog, and sky atmosphere. Editor tooling includes a content browser and KindUI; subsystems cover scene entities, terrain streaming/foliage, text shaping, and cross-platform support via SDL3 (Windows, Linux, macOS, Android, iOS). (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/udinmoInc__WindEffects.md)

Sits in the Game Engine / Vulkan source lane—aimed at developers and engineers studying modern engine architecture, graphics, and tooling internals relevant to game-security research, rather than as a cheat or anti-cheat artifact.
