# r2morph

Metamorphic binary transformation engine built on r2pipe and radare2 (Python + C). Centers on modding, memory analysis, and debugging; aimed at game-security / RE research in the Cheat → Radare Plugins lane. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/seifreed__r2morph.md)

Useful as an r2-driven metamorphic/mutation reference alongside polymorphic shredders and PE obfuscators—not a commercial protector or unpacker.
