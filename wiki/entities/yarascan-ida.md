# yarascan-ida

Python IDA plugin for scanning files with Yara rules. Aimed at game-security researchers and reverse engineers in the cheat / IDA Plugins lane studying signature-based triage during static RE. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/senko37__yarascan-ida.md)

IDA-side Yara scan helper—not a full AV engine or unpacker framework.
