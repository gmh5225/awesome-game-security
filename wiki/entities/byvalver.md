# byvalver

Shellcode **bad-byte banisher** that preserves payload functionality. Generic bad-byte banishment framework with two usage modes. Aimed at anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers studying shellcode engine & tricks (constrained encodings / forbidden-byte avoidance) rather than shipping as an AC product. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/umpolungfish__byvalver.md)

Complements Windows shellcode build frameworks such as [[scfw]] and in-process asm/shellcode prototyping such as [[quickasm]].
