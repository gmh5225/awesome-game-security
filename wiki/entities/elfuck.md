# elfuck

Linux ELF binary packer/protector (C) with executable compression, encryption, and anti-debugging. Implements NRV2E decompression, password-based locking, runtime ELF loading, and self-modifying code. Aimed at binary-protection researchers studying ELF packing and Linux executable obfuscation under Anti Cheat → Binary Packer / `[ELF]`, rather than shipping as an AC product. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/timhsutw__elfuck.md)

Useful as a Linux/ELF packer reference alongside [[woody-woodpacker]] and PE packing tooling—not a full unpacker or debugger.
