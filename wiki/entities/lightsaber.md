# lightsaber

iOS 18.4–18.6.2 userland exploit chain that injects JavaScript into SpringBoard and other processes (runtime JS modification). Derived from DarkSword. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/zeroxjf__lightsaber.md)

Useful for mobile game-security and reverse-engineering research on offensive iOS jailbreak / cheat-adjacent techniques: userland exploit chaining and process-targeted JS injection without treating the chain as a production jailbreak product.
