# proxmox-ve-anti-detection

Kernel-oriented research project for **hidden PVE** (Proxmox VE): reducing or masking virtualization fingerprints so a QEMU/KVM/PVE guest looks less like a VM to anti-cheat and environment checks. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/zhaodice__proxmox-ve-anti-detection.md)

Useful for game-security researchers and reverse engineers studying offensive techniques in the `Cheat > QEMU/KVM/PVE/VBOX` lane and the defensive counterpart `Detection:Virtual Environments` / hacked-hypervisor surfaces—not a production AC component.
