# OFRP-device_xiaomi_mondrian

OrangeFox Recovery Project (OFRP) device tree for the Xiaomi Redmi K60 Pro (codename mondrian). Supplies recovery partition config, TWRP flags, a prebuilt kernel with Goodix fingerprint module, and vendor HAL binaries (gatekeeper, keymaster, QSEECOM) needed to boot custom recovery on Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/ymdzq__OFRP-device_xiaomi_mondrian.md)

Relevant to Android game-security research on custom recovery / bootloader / ROM paths that underpin Magisk-style root and device-side analysis on Snapdragon handsets.
