# IDARustDemangler

IDA-oriented Rust demangler & normalizer. Demangling is driven by the `rs-dml` binary (must be installed separately). Aimed at game-security researchers and reverse engineers in the cheat / IDA Plugins lane working with Rust-mangled symbols. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/timetravelthree__IDARustDemangler.md)

Not a full RE suite—scoped as symbol-name recovery / normalization for Rust binaries in IDA.
