# x64-EXE-Packer

C/C++ PE X64 binary packer listed under Anti Cheat → Binary Packer. Aimed at anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers studying packed x64 PE clients rather than shipping as an AC product. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/xsj3n__x64-EXE-Packer.md)

Useful as an x64 packer reference alongside PE32 packing and PE triage tooling—not a full unpacker or debugger.
