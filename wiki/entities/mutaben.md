# mutaben

Simple mixed-boolean-arithmetic (MBA) expression generator written in Python. Useful for game-security researchers and reverse engineers studying cheat-side MBA obfuscation (generate samples to exercise simplification / RE workflows). (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/z1ko__mutaben.md)

Not a deobfuscator—generation-only tooling for the Cheat Mixed boolean-arithmetic lane.
