# AndroidCheatTemplate

Android Unity cheat framework in C/C++ centered on OpenGL, Unity, and hooking—aimed at researchers studying offensive techniques in the cheat / game engine explorer:Unity lane. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/sanqiuu__AndroidCheatTemplate.md)

Complements GLES ImGui Unity mod-menu templates ([[imgui-unity]], [[imgui-unity-with-layout]]), Kotlin/Java floating-menu + JNI paths ([[android-mod-menu-kotlin]], [[android-modmenu-semijni]]), and Android native ImGui apps ([[android-native-app-imgui]]).
