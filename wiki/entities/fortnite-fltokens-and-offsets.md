# Fortnite-Fltokens-and-offsets

Fortnite-oriented grabber for FLTokens and related offsets (versions 1–2 noted as usable; author has not fully tested). Framed for cheat / game-security research on Fortnite rather than as a maintained product—the repo states it will no longer update and may go offline. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/zinx-YT__Fortnite-Fltokens-and-offsets.md)

Treat as a historical snapshot of Unreal/Fortnite offset and token-lookup material for RE and threat modeling—not a live offset feed.
