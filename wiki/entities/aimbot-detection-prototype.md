# aimbot-detection-prototype

Deep-learning **prototype for aimbot / triggerbot detection**. While running it shows a window of the gameplay under analysis plus a count of recorded clips. Useful for anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers in the `Detection:Triggerbot & Aimbot` lane. Sibling to the later CS2-focused [[waldo]] project from the same org. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/waldo-vision__aimbot-detection-prototype.md)
