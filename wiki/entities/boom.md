# BOOM

Research sample that hijacks `Beep.sys`, then hides and alters its communication path—useful for studying how cheats/drivers may avoid obvious IOCTL or named-device surfaces. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/zoand__BOOM.md)

Aimed at game-security and reverse-engineering researchers in the cheat / driver-communication lane—not a production stack.
