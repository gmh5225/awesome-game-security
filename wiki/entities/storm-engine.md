# Storm Engine

Open-sourced C++ game engine originally developed by Akella for the Sea Dogs / Pirates of the Caribbean series. Includes a DirectX 9 renderer, physics, scripting, resource management, scene graph, water simulation, and ship-combat mechanics—a complete early-2000s commercial engine tree for historians, hobbyist developers, and vintage engine-architecture research. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/storm-devs__storm-engine.md)

Sits in the Game Engine / source lane (adjacent DirectX Compatibility / DX9 study)—OSS commercial engine code for development and RE study, rather than a cheat or anti-cheat artifact.
