# csgo-external-cheat

External CS:GO cheat research sample (C++). Out-of-process memory reads (ReadProcessMemory or a kernel driver) for ESP, aimbot, radar, and basic visuals—entity positions, health, and weapon data—without injecting into the CS:GO process. Aimed at researchers studying external cheat patterns and anti-cheat external detection. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/rrpvm__csgo-external-cheat.md)

Treat as an RPM / driver-backed external counterpart to Internal CS:GO baselines—not a feature checklist.
