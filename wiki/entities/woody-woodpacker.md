# woody_woodpacker

ELF-focused binary packer that produces a packed program named “woody” from an input executable. Aimed at anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers studying packed ELF clients under Anti Cheat → Binary Packer, rather than shipping as an AC product. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/vsteffen__woody_woodpacker.md)

Useful as a Linux/ELF packer reference alongside PE32/X64 packing tooling—not a full unpacker or debugger.
