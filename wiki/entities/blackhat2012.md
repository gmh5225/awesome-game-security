# blackhat2012

Black Hat 2012 sample suite (rrbranco) of anti-analysis PoCs in four categories: Anti-Debugging, Anti-Disassembly, Obfuscation, and Anti-VM. Anti-debugging samples ship as C/C++ (Visual Studio 2010, `Csrc`) and Flat Assembler (`ASMsrc`). Useful reference for anti-cheat engineers and defensive researchers studying classic anti-debug / anti-disasm / obfuscation / anti-VM techniques. (source: wiki/sources/descriptions/rrbranco__blackhat2012.md)

Complements modern Anti Debugging catalogs such as [[makin]], RE-tool blind spots such as [[hint-break]], and VE-detection demos such as [[anticuckoo]].
