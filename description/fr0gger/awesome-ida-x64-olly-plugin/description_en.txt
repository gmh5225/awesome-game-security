A curated awesome list of plugins and scripts for major reverse-engineering tools, including IDA Pro, Ghidra, x64dbg, OllyDbg, and GDB. Entries cover patching, binary diffing, deobfuscation, emulation, code coverage, YARA scanning, crypto constant finding, driver and heap analysis, and anti-anti-debug helpers. Many listed projects are Python- or IDAPython-based and integrate frameworks such as Unicorn, Keystone, angr, and ChatGPT-assisted decompilation helpers. The collection is aimed at reverse engineers, malware analysts, exploit developers, and game-security researchers who extend these disassemblers and debuggers for binary analysis and vulnerability research.
