Multi Theft Auto: San Andreas (MTA:SA) is an open-source multiplayer modification for Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas, providing full client and dedicated server stacks for custom online gameplay. It is written primarily in C++ with extensive Lua scripting for resources and mods, and uses Premake-based builds across Windows and Linux. The client embeds Chromium (CEF) for in-game browsers, hooks Direct3D and DirectInput for rendering and input control, and includes anti-cheat modules plus utilities such as hook/DLL analysis tooling. The project is aimed at multiplayer game development and game-security research around GTA San Andreas modding, client integrity, and anti-cheat techniques.
